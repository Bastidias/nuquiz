## Items that don't yield a clean comparable group

- **1.7 Concepts of Management — "managing resources."** The NCSBN Related Content language ("manage resources, personnel, and information") is broad and doesn't itself yield Rows × Columns. Resource-allocation specifics are captured via leadership styles (1.7) and prioritization frameworks (1.10). If SME wants a dedicated Concept, propose `resource-allocation-decisions.md` as Aspects.
- **1.13 Information Technology.** Most of NCSBN's IT content is normative (use EHR correctly, protect credentials, retrieve data). Captured as one Aspects Concept; expanding to Dimensions would force comparison Rows that don't naturally exist.
- **1.16 Referrals.** No standalone Concept; the comparable group lives inside 1.6 (which roles, when, for what trigger).
